Our Story

A Lafayette Tradition Since 1991

Cajun Sno has been serving up stuffed snowballs, smiles, and summer memories to Lafayette families for over three decades.

From One Stand to a Local Favorite

Family-owned & operated • 35+ years

Cajun Sno was opened in 1991 by Robin Chevalier with a simple idea: serve the best-tasting snowballs in Lafayette with the kind of friendly service you’d expect from family.

Since then, Cajun Sno has become a seasonal tradition. Kids who grew up visiting the stand now bring their own families to share the same cool, colorful treats and laid-back Louisiana vibes.

More than just a snowball stand. Cajun Sno is where ball games are celebrated, summer days are cooled down, and neighbors become regulars.
